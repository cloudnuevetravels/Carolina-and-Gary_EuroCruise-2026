London Know-Before-You-Go ›
Oyster Card or Contactless: Tap your contactless credit/debit card directly on TfL readers — no Oyster card needed. Cheaper than cash tickets.
Tipping: 10–12.5% at restaurants is standard. Many add a service charge automatically — check the bill before tipping again.
Pub etiquette: Order and pay at the bar, not at the table. Drinks are served in pints (568ml) and half-pints.
Queues are sacred: Brits take queuing very seriously. Always join the back — no exceptions!
Weather: August averages 20–23°C (68–73°F). Bring a light jacket — it can rain at any time.
Getting around: The Tube is fastest. The Elizabeth Line connects Heathrow directly to central London in comfort.
Basic Economy note: Delta Basic Economy has restrictions on seat selection and changes. Check your specific ticket terms on the Delta app before flying.
Virgin Voyages Tips ›
All dining is included — no surcharges at any of the 20+ restaurants. Only premium bottles of wine/spirits cost extra.
Sailor app = your cruise remote: Book restaurants for the whole trip as soon as you board. Popular slots fill up on Day 1.
